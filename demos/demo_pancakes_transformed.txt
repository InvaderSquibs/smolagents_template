Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes (gluten-free)

Dietary Restrictions: gluten-free

Serves 8

Substitutions Made:
  1. all-purpose flour → almond flour
     Note: High protein, low carb. May need more liquid in recipes.

Ingredients:
• 2 tbsp granulated sugar
• 2 tsp baking powder
• 1 tsp baking soda
• 1 tsp salt
• 2 large eggs
• 2 cups buttermilk
• 1/4 cup melted butter 1/4 cup melted butter
• 1 tsp vanilla extract
• 2 cups almond flour

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
2. In another bowl, beat eggs with buttermilk, melted butter, and vanilla.
3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
4. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat.
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
6. Cook until bubbles form on surface, then flip.
7. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
