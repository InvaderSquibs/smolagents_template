Chocolate Layer Cake (vegan, keto, nut-free)

Dietary Restrictions: vegan, keto, nut-free

Serves 12

Substitutions Made:
  1. all-purpose flour → almond flour
     Note: High protein, low carb. May need more liquid.
  2. eggs → flax egg
     Note: Mix and let sit 5 minutes. Good for binding.
  3. milk → oat milk
     Note: Creamy texture, neutral flavor. Good for coffee and baking.
  4. 1/2 cup butter, softened → coconut oil
     Note: Solid at room temperature. Good for baking.
  5. 1/3 cup milk → oat milk
     Note: Creamy texture, neutral flavor. Good for coffee and baking.

Ingredients:
• 2 cups granulated sugar
• 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
• 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
• 1 tsp salt
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil 1/2 cup vegetable oil
• 2 tsp vanilla extract
• 1 cup boiling water
• 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 3 cups powdered sugar
• 1 tsp vanilla extract
• 2 1/4 cups almond flour
• 2 large flax egg
• 1 cup oat milk
• 1/2 cup coconut oil
• 1/3 cup oat milk

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
3. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes.
5. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
6. Pour into prepared pans.
7. Bake for 30-35 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
8. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks.
9. For frosting, beat butter and cocoa until smooth.
10. Gradually beat in powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla.
11. Frost cooled cake layers.
