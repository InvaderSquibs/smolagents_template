Caesar Salad with Croutons

Ingredients:
1 large head romaine lettuce
1/2 cup olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 large eggs
1/4 cup croutons
2 anchovy fillets, minced

Instructions:
1. Wash and tear lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, Parmesan, lemon juice, mustard, Worcestershire, salt, and pepper.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs until frothy.
4. Gradually whisk egg mixture into dressing.
5. Add anchovies and whisk until smooth.
6. Toss lettuce with dressing.
7. Top with croutons and additional Parmesan.

Serves 4
