Classic Quiche Lorraine

Ingredients:
1 9-inch pie crust
6 large eggs
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp nutmeg
8 oz bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 1/2 cups shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Place pie crust in pie pan and crimp edges.
3. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
4. Sprinkle bacon, cheese, and onion evenly over pie crust.
5. Pour egg mixture over filling.
6. Bake for 35-40 minutes until center is set.
7. Let cool for 10 minutes before slicing.

Serves 8
